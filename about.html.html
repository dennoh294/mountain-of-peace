About Us

Mountain Of Peace Worship Centre.

The Church was established by the vision bearer Rev. Kenyatta in 2011 after serving for many years as an Evangelist and under other men of GOD.
Since then the church has grown immensely in terms of infrastructural development,quantity, mission works and recently launching of Mountain of peace television and Witikio fm.
The Church has extended it's ministries by opening branches countrywide such as witeithie and Mwihoko branch
The Church holds mission work all over the country every year, transversing towns and cities spreading the Gospel, making God Known to all cities in Kenya Recently mission team held mission work in juja, kiambu county and kenol, Muranga county.
